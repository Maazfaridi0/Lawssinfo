MAINTENANCE OF WIFE AND CHILDREN UNDER CRPC, 1973

MAINTENANCE OF WIFE AND CHILDREN UNDER CRPC, 1973

In our Constitution there are so many fundamental rights and duties, one of them there is a fundamental duty of a man or husband to maintain his wife, children and as well as parents till then when they are not able to maintain themselves. In our Hindu Succession Act, 1956 it is mention in Atharva Veda that a husband should always love and care his wife and give her the respect, he should not be rude to his wife and support his wife even in the difficult times. Apart from that our parents should have major responsibility to teach their children and teach them and supporting them emotionally and as well as physically. Like that, it is the duty of any person to take care their parents either it is wife’s Parents or Husband’s Parents. According to our Dharma our parents are the most important part of our life.


In today’s generation, children’s doesn’t take care about their parents, wife and children when they married. Not app people are like that, but it has been seen that people are becoming self-cared. This research paper deals what are the duties of a person towards his wife and children.
